Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor Since 1965

Welcome to Shudh Gadbad Chaat, where tradition meets taste. For over five decades, we have been serving the most authentic and delicious chaat items to our cherished customers, keeping the recipes and techniques passed down through generations.

Our journey began in 1965 with a simple dream: to bring the authentic flavors of Indian street food to everyone's table. Today, we continue that legacy with the same passion, dedication, and commitment to quality that we started with.

At Shudh Gadbad Chaat, we believe that great food brings people together. Every dish we prepare is made with love, fresh ingredients, and time-honored recipes that capture the true essence of Indian cuisine.
